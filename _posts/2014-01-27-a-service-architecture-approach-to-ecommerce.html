When it comes to ecommerce, one size does not fit all.Since 2007, weve been building a flexible ecommerce solution for develpers and have come across just about every possible need you can imagine.The number of different ways people want to sell things online is mind-numbing.Trying to fit that all into a turnkey solution rarely works.Instead, weve found the best approach is often to use the best of breed solutions working closely together in a service oreiented architecture (SOA).
