By Darrel Miller on January 23, 2015.Hypermedia APIs are beginning to enter the mainstream of API development. For developers trying to get up to speed on hypermedia there are a dizzying array of terminology and choices.This blog post gives an overview of the hypermedia space, giving some perspective on where we have come from, what has been tried, what has worked, what has not, where the industry is headed and what questions remain unresolved. Read more here.
